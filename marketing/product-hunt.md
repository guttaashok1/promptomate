## Posting checklist

- [ ] Account has been around for more than 2 weeks (PH deprioritizes new accounts)
- [ ] At least 5 hunted products on your profile before launching your own (or ask a maker-in-residence to hunt you)
- [ ] Ship page assets ready: logo (1024x1024 PNG), gallery images
- [ ] First comment drafted (above)
- [ ] Twitter post planned for 9 AM PT to drive external traffic
- [ ] Discord/Slack community DMs queued (close friends who'll upvote in the first hour — top 5 products in the first 4 hours dominate the whole day)
- [ ] Ready to reply to every comment for 12+ hours

## Don't

- Don't ask strangers to upvote. PH detects voting rings and will shadow-rank you to oblivion.
- Don't launch the same day as a huge competitor (check PH the day before).
- Don't post just a URL — write the first comment.
